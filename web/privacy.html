Privacy Policy

Last updated 18 July 2026

Amber is built around a simple promise: what you share stays yours. Your health details and everything Amber remembers live on your device, and the choices you make about what Amber may use are always yours to change.

What we hold, and where

Your profile, weigh-ins, food logs, habits, and the facts Amber remembers are stored on your device. We do not keep a copy of them on our servers, and we do not sell them, ever.

What leaves your device

  • To answer you in chat or in a live conversation, the message you send and the context you have consented to share are sent to our AI providers (Runware and OpenAI) purely to generate a reply. They are not used to train models.
  • Speech-to-text for typing by voice runs on your device.
  • Nothing else is transmitted unless you explicitly export or share it.

Your consent controls

In Settings you decide what Amber may use. You can, at any time and at no cost to the core service:

  • Stop personal facts from ever reaching the model.
  • Turn off Amber reaching out to you first.
  • Stop sharing support signals with your care team.

Turning any of these off never limits the service you paid for. A consent you cannot refuse without losing what you paid for is not freely given.

The eMed programme

Amber accompanies an eMed weight-loss programme. Clinical care, prescriptions, and any at-home test results are handled by eMed under their own privacy policy. Amber only reflects what you have shared with it.

Your rights

You can view everything Amber holds at any time, export it from Settings, or delete your account — which permanently removes your profile and Amber's memory from your device.

Children

Amber is intended for adults enrolled in an eMed programme and is not directed at anyone under 18.

Changes

If this policy changes in a way that affects you, we will update this page and note the date above.

Contact

Questions about your privacy? Email support@emed.com.
